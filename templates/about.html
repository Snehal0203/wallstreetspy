About us

"The perfect place to enjoy life and food "

Le café vintage has been totally involved in the business of coffee from growing, processing, manufacturing, transporting, distributing and selling the products to the consumer. In tune with the global and domestic Market demands. Le café vintage is very flexible that it provides a wide range of products that cater to the taste of every individual market and consumer to enrich the lives of our consumer and allow them to make environment and socially conscious choice. The company is constantly upgrading its production facilities and capacities. By constantly adding new ranges to its already burgeoned portfolio, Le café vintage is poised to become a major player in the global coffee market. From manually operated machines in the early days the company has graduated to high – tech machinery in its pursuit for excellence.
